How to Use OpenGrammar

1

📝 Write or Paste Your Text

Start by typing or pasting your text into the main text area. OpenGrammar supports documents up to 10,000 characters.

2

🔍 Click "Check Grammar"

Hit the "Check Grammar" button to analyze your text. Our AI will scan for grammar errors, style issues, and readability problems.

3

✅ Review and Accept Suggestions

Click on highlighted issues to see detailed explanations and suggested corrections. Use "Accept" to apply changes or "Dismiss" to ignore.

4

📖 Find Synonyms

Click "Find Synonyms" button, then select any word to see alternative word suggestions. Click on a synonym to replace the original word.

5

📊 View Analytics

Check the analysis panel for readability scores, word count, sentence structure insights, and detailed writing metrics.

6

📄 Export Your Document

Once satisfied with your text, use the "Export to PDF" button to download your improved document as a professional PDF.

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

Ctrl + Enter Check Grammar
Alt + S Toggle Synonym Mode
Escape Close Tooltips/Exit Modes
Ctrl + A Select All Text

💡 Pro Tips

  • Context Matters: Our AI considers the context of your writing for more accurate suggestions.
  • Style Options: Different writing styles (academic, casual, business) may have different requirements.
  • Review Carefully: Always review suggestions before accepting them to maintain your unique voice.
  • Continuous Learning: The more you use OpenGrammar, the better it becomes at understanding your writing style.
